Music Information Retrieval at Boise State University

Music Information Retrieval (MIR) research at Boise State University is currently focused on the problem of Automatic Music Transcription (AMT). The goal is to allow a person with a personal mobile device such as a cell phone or tablet computer to capture a monophonic signal of a live music performance in a noisy environment and convert that signal to MIDI or standard western music notation files for each of the individual instruments.
